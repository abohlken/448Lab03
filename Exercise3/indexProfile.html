Doge: The Meme, The Myth, The Legend

"On February 23rd, 2010, Japanese kindergarten teacher Atsuko Sato posted several photos of her rescue-adopted Shiba Inu dog Kabosu to her personal blog. Among the photos included a peculiar shot of Kabosu sitting on a couch while glaring sideways at the camera with raised eyebrows." (source: knowyourmeme.com)

Ever since Doge's debut on the internet, nothing's ever been the same. Such an impact one dog can make, especially since this joke hurts no one. It's a joke that everyone can enjoy, which is why it was one of the first big internet memes.
